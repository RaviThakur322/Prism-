Paper Submission Guidelines and Plagiarism Policy

  • Submitted papers must comply with plagiarism policy and procedures i.e. plagiarism must not be more than 15% in the paper and not more than 1% from a single source.
  • Only papers having genuine content with original research results will be considered for publication.
  • Papers submitted to HCICC - 25 must not have been published elsewhere and must not be under review or submitted for review elsewhere while under consideration for HCICC - 25.
  • HCICC - 25 technical programme committee will look for all ethical standards in the best possible way; further authors will be responsible for any plagiarism claims.
  • Kindly make sure the paper will be as per Springer guidelines. Please go through the instructions and springer format:https://www.springer.com/gp/authors-editors/book-authors-editors/your-publication-journey/manuscript-preparation
